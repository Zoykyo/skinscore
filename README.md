personalized protocol. Every intervention is scored on one scale (**SkinScore**) and ranked.

## The companion app

personalized 🟢/🟡/🔴 fit engine, a dashboard, progress photos, and the full 90-item leaderboard. Once this repo is
pushed with GitHub Pages enabled (Actions), it deploys automatically to:

**https://zoykyo.github.io/skinscore/** — open on your phone and *Add to Home Screen*.

The Pages workflow ([`.github/workflows/pages.yml`](.github/workflows/pages.yml)) regenerates the app's data from the
Markdown below on every push, so the app always reflects the latest research. See [`app/README.md`](app/README.md).

## The headline

Prevention beats correction and it's cheap: sun protection, not smoking, barrier care, sleep, and a
low-glycemic diet top the board. A few proven correctors (retinoids, in-clinic procedures) actually
reverse damage. The futuristic frontier — exosomes, stem cells, senolytics, genetic tests — is sold
years ahead of its evidence and sits at the bottom. Full reasoning in Cycle 9.

## What's next
